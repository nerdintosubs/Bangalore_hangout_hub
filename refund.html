Cancellation & Refund Policy

Effective: 05 Jan 2026

Cancellations

  • Free cancellation up to 2 hours before the scheduled start time.
  • Within 2 hours of the session, a nominal late-cancellation fee may apply to cover therapist travel/time.
  • No-shows may be required to prepay for future bookings.

Rescheduling

You may reschedule up to 2 hours before the session without charges, subject to therapist availability.

Refunds

  • If any advance/prepayment was made and the session is canceled by us, we will refund the amount in full within 3–5 business days via original mode (UPI/bank transfer).
  • If you cancel within 2 hours of the scheduled time, any prepayment may be partially refunded at our discretion.

Quality & Safety

If you experience any issue, contact us immediately on WhatsApp at +91 9187351205. We will investigate and offer a fair resolution per our standards.
